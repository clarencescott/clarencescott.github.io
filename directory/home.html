Who is Clarence Scott?


I am a passionate and dedicated technology enthusiast with a track record of driving innovation and positive change in various communities. Through initiatives such as Destination Innovation, I've been involved in STEM and STEAM programs aimed at transforming the technological landscape in Flint, MI, by fostering creativity and knowledge sharing.


At the MSU Appathon hosted at Michigan State University - Flint campus, I contributed to the development of applications designed to empower healthcare professionals with vital research tools for delivering optimal patient care and documentation.


These experiences reflect my commitment to leveraging technology for the betterment of my community and individuals alike.
