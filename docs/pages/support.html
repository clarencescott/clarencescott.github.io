Computer Support & Troubleshooting

Quick Fixes for Common Problems

  • Wi-Fi not working?
    Try restarting your router and computer. Also ensure Airplane Mode is turned off.
  • Screen flickering or black?
    Update your graphics driver or boot into Safe Mode to troubleshoot display settings.
  • Computer making loud noises?
    This usually means your fan is clogged with dust. Clean with compressed air or let us take a look.
  • Blue screen (BSOD) errors?
    Make note of the error code and contact us. Often related to driver or memory issues.
  • Slow boot times?
    Disable unnecessary startup programs using Task Manager & run disk cleanup.
  • Can’t install software?
    Make sure you're running the installer as Administrator and that your system meets the software’s requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is my computer running slow?
    Could be caused by background apps, low RAM, malware, or an aging hard drive.
  • Can you help with password recovery?
    Yes, we can assist with resetting or recovering Windows, Mac, and email passwords safely.
  • How do I avoid viruses?
    Avoid clicking unknown links, keep antivirus up to date, and don’t download cracked software.
  • Is remote support available?
    Yes! We offer secure remote access support for diagnostics and software issues.
  • Do you fix Apple devices?
    Yes, we handle MacBooks and iMac issues including startup errors, OS reinstall, and hardware problems.
